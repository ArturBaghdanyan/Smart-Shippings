QUESTIONS & ANSWERS

Frequently Asked Questions

How I should pack my goods?

It is important to pack(wrap) your goods properly. If a shipment is not properly packed, there is a risk of getting damaged. Also, it can damage other goods.

What should I do if my shipment arrived damaged?

If goods are lost or damaged during transport and damage is visible, please make a note on the waybill or transfer documents when your shipment is received. Also, contact us.

How long will transportation by road take?

The transit times can vary depending on your goods freight forwarding departure and destination points.
